Our Approach

The Lauter Tun

In brewing, the lauter tun is the vessel with a false bottom — separating what's valuable from what isn't, letting clarity rise to the surface.

It's an apt metaphor for what we do. Complex situations — distressed balance sheets, operational inefficiencies, unclear strategy — require the same discipline: patient separation of signal from noise, structure from chaos, potential from liability.

We bring decades of operating and financial experience to bear on exactly that problem. Not theoretical frameworks, but hands-on work alongside management teams to identify what's working, fix what isn't, and build a clear path forward.

Clarity

We cut through complexity to identify the core issues — financial, operational, and strategic.

Expertise

Seasoned professionals with direct experience in restructuring, M&A, and financial leadership.

Partnership

We work alongside your team, not above it — embedding in your organization to drive real change.

About the Firm

Built for the
Lower Middle Market.

Lautertun Partners is a specialized advisory firm focused exclusively on lower middle market companies — businesses with $5M–$50M in revenue that require serious expertise but are underserved by large advisory firms.

Our principals have guided companies through every stage of the business lifecycle: from crisis stabilization to growth, from founder succession to institutional sale. We understand the urgency, complexity, and human weight of these situations.

We are operators as well as partners. We have sat in the CFO seat, led creditor negotiations, closed acquisitions, and restructured organizations. When we advise, it is from experience — not from a playbook.
